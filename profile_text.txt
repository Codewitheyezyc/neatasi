About Neatasi
Nea Tasi Oil and Gas Services is a dynamic, forward-looking multinational corporation headquartered
in Nigeria, with operations spanning the United Arab Emirates and South Sudan. Founded in 2024,
Neatasi has rapidly positioned itself as a key player across Africa and the Middle East, offering a broad
spectrum of energy, natural resources, and infrastructure solutions.
With a leadership team of seasoned professionals and industry experts, Neatasi operates at the
intersection of energy security, resource development, and sustainable infrastructure — bringing
world-class expertise to emerging and established markets alike.

Our Services & Business Lines
Neatasi operates across ten strategic business verticals, enabling the company to deliver integrated solutions across the
energy and resources value chain.
Oil & Gas Exploration Neatasi conducts upstream oil and gas exploration activities across
sub-Saharan Africa, leveraging advanced geological surveys and
strategic partnerships to identify and develop viable hydrocarbon
reserves.
Gas Supply We supply processed and piped natural gas to industrial, commercial,
and governmental clients, ensuring reliable energy access across our
operational territories.
Pipeline Construction Our engineering division specialises in the design, construction, and
maintenance of oil and gas pipeline infrastructure, adhering to
international safety and quality standards.
Fuel Distribution Neatasi manages end-to-end fuel distribution networks, supplying
refined petroleum products to downstream markets across Nigeria and
South Sudan.
Crude Oil Trading We engage in international crude oil trading, connecting producers with
global refineries through transparent, efficient, and commercially sound
trading operations.
Drilling Services Our drilling operations support both exploration and production phases,
employing modern rig technologies and experienced technical crews.
Gold Mining & Trading Neatasi holds mining interests and conducts artisanal and large-scale
gold mining operations, with an active gold trading desk serving local
and international buyers.
Construction &
Engineering
From civil infrastructure to industrial facility development, our
construction arm delivers turnkey projects on time and within budget.
Dredging We provide professional dredging services for waterway maintenance,
land reclamation, and offshore construction — critical in Nigeria's Niger
Delta and South Sudan's river systems.
Haulage We manage end-to-end heavy-duty logistics and bulk haulage services,
transporting heavy equipment, natural resources, and petroleum products
across our operational corridors.
